⚠️ Important Setup

To enable professional invoice generation and ensure accurate business records, Fox Ledger requires your company and payment details to be configured.

  • 🏢 Navigate to Settings and enter your company information
  • 🏦 Add up to three bank accounts for payment flexibility
  • 📲 Include your Shop2Shop account and barcode for invoice display
  • 🧾 These details will be automatically applied to generated invoices

✔ Completing this setup is required before generating PDF invoices
